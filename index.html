For MBA & Master's Candidates
Why join Peetslist?
We're not just another internship. Peetslist is a practicum for aspiring strategy consultants — MBA and Master's students alike — with real clients, real deliverables, and a track record of MBB placements.

Live Client Projects

Work on actual engagements with Fortune Global 500 enterprises. Hypothesis-driven consulting on real business problems.

MBB-Track Record

Our alumni have received offers from Strategy&, McKinsey, and Bain. Build the exact skills recruiters look for: hypothesis building, client communication, and deliverable crafting.

Structured Training

4-domain, 3-level skill map covering research, analysis, communication, and project management. Systematic OJT with weekly feedback loops.

World-Class Mentor Network

Our advisory board includes professionals from McKinsey, IDEO, and VC/PE backgrounds. Their insights shape our methodology and project reviews — elevating the quality of every engagement you work on.

Flexible & Remote

Up to 20 hours/week, fully remote from anywhere in the world. Designed to fit around your MBA coursework — not compete with it.

Paid Experience

Competitive compensation from day one. While the real value is career acceleration, you're fairly compensated for every hour you contribute.
